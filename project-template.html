Ladies, Home Page Redesign

Role UI/UX Designer
Timeline 3 Weeks
Tools Figma, Adobe Illustrator

The Challenge

The client approached me with an outdated website that had a high bounce rate. The goal was to completely overhaul the user experience, making it more intuitive for their target audience while modernizing the aesthetic to match their new brand guidelines.

My Approach

I started by conducting user research to understand where the current friction points were. From there, I developed wireframes and low-fidelity prototypes. Once the structure was approved, I moved into high-fidelity design, focusing on a dark-mode theme with vibrant neon accents to make the calls-to-action pop.

The Result

After launching the new design, the client saw a 40% increase in user retention and a significant bump in conversion rates. The interface is now fully responsive, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.
