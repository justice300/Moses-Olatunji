UX Research & Interface Design

Habitual

A deep dive into the branding and user experience of daily essentials.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

4 Weeks

Project

E-Commerce

Tools

Figma, Adobe CC

Visual Concept

Habitual is designed to provide a high-end, clean aesthetic for the everyday shopper, focusing on clarity and ease of use.

Project Overview

Defining the core mission: bridging the gap between luxury branding and the practical needs of grocery and home shopping.

The Problem Space

Identifying key friction points in the user journey, such as information overload and complex checkout flows.

Design Solutions

Implementing strategic fixes like personalized category filtering and a simplified, one-page transaction process.

Information Architecture

Mapping out a seamless sitemap to ensure that every product is reachable within three taps of the home screen.

Style Guide & Branding

Establishing a vibrant visual foundation using a signature gold accent, robust sans-serif typography, and custom iconography.

Target Audience & Research

Developing deep-dive personas to understand the specific shopping habits and frustrations of our core user demographics.

User Personas

Mapping out emotional and functional needs to ensure the UI design addresses the diverse lifestyles of Habitual's users.

High-Fidelity Mockups

The final design implementation showcasing the fluid navigation and high-contrast interface across the entire app ecosystem.
