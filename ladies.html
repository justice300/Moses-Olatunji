E-Commerce Redesign

Ladies. Home Page Redesign

A minimalist approach to high-fashion retail interface.

Role

Lead UI/UX Designer

Timeline

2 Weeks

Tools

Figma, Photoshop

Goal

Increase conversion through visual hierarchy.

The Challenge

The original interface felt cluttered. I focused on white space and "breathing room" to let the product photography speak for itself. By using a clean serif typeface for categories and a bold sans-serif for CTAs, I created a balanced visual hierarchy.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Hero Section: Clear value proposition with an "Explore Now" trigger.
  • Minimalist Navigation: Hidden search and cart to reduce cognitive load.
  • Visual Categories: High-quality imagery for Clothing, Shoes, and Bags for intuitive browsing.
